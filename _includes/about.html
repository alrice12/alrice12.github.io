About

Well, science is all about communicating your results to laypeople in the same way that tech writing requires conveying information efficiently, accurately, and in a manner digestable to the intended audience.

I've managed a range of scientific writing and documentation, from proposals to peer-reviewed publications.

I'm a detail oriented writer and editor and my desire to learn is my motivation. I enjoy working as part of a team, particularly with people who are similarly motivated.
